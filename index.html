I am interested in deep generative models and disentanglement learning, especially Generative Adversarial Networks. I also follow multiple tracks of robotic application, such as robot perception, multi-agent collaboration and autonomous driving.

Hardware Projects

Air Hockey Robot

Jun 13, 2015

Air hockey is a popular table game, and this robot can play this game with human beings. The bird-view camera captures the position of the ball and the computer send the coordinates to the controller of three step motors. Then, the controller drive the mechanical arm with a loop feedback.


Coorperation Mobile Robot Platform

Jul 18, 2016

This is a platform equipped with Ultra Wide Band (UWB) for coorperative robot navigation. The robots communicate with each other with UWB, and they can dynamically form a pre-defined formation with the distance information. These robots are entirely self-made and controlled by STM32 micro-chip.


Micro Drone

Jul 15, 2017

This is a micro drone built based on a piece of PCB. Its software controller is on the basis of PID, and it can be remotely controlled by an Android APP with Bluetooth connection. The size of this drone is about 9cm*9cm, and the battery can last more than 10 minites.


BLE Localization System

Jul 18, 2017

This a set of equipment that utilize received signal strength indicator (RSSI) of Bluetooth to measure the distance between the source and receive devices. With reasonable layout on the ceiling, these devices can work as a positioning system to provide location of source devices.


3D Reconstruction with RGBD Camera

Jul 3, 2017

This platform utilizes RGBD sensors (ASUS Xtion and Realsense) to reconstruct rooms and objects with raw pointcloud. The algorithm is a basic SLAM method that consists of a frond-end of ORB and a back-end of G2O. Code is avaliable here.
